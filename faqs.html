Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mesh WiFi?

WiFi Mesh networks are an innovative new way to extend your WiFi coverage, no matter where you live. They eliminate dead zones and provide stronger signals throughout the whole home!

How does Mesh WiFi work?

With Unifi, you can blanket your entire home with fast WiFi while only needing one name and password. As Mesh nodes are placed throughout the house to provide connectivity wherever people are located - even outside on or above ground level--they will automatically switch devices over as needed so there's no disruption in speed!

Does this replace my existing WiFi?

Unifi Mesh units replace your current router, therefore, your current WiFi.

Does this replace my modem or router?

Unifi Mesh replaces your old router and works with your modem to blanket your home in fast, stable, and secure WiFi from the bedroom to the backyard.

Does Mesh WiFi work with my exisiting internet provider?

Yes, Unifi Mesh WiFi works with all Internet Service Providers (ISPs) and modems.

How many devices can be connected to a Mesh WiFi network?

Depending on the Unifi Mesh WiFi system you choose, you can connect up to 150 smart home, gaming, and streaming devices. Each Unifi Mesh system will provide information on how many devices you can connect based on the pack size.

How many Mesh WiFi units can I have in my home?

It is best to connect 10 or fewer Unifi units in your home to maintain better performance; however, there is no limit on the number of Unifi units you can add to your network.

Can I used a wired Ethernet connection instead of WiFi to get the units to commnicate with each other?

Absolutely. Unifi systems allow you to create a stronger and more robust backbone for your network by connecting your Unifi units using an Ethernet cable.
