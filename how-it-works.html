Traditional WiFi can be clunky

With a whole home mesh WiFi system from Unifi, you will no longer experience dropped WiFi connections as you move through your home. This is what a seamless network is all about.

Smooth, seamless technology

One name, one password, smooth

Unifi systems use WiFi nodes that work together to cast a stronger and more reliable WiFi signal throughout your whole home, allowing you to move freely from room to room.

Ranch-style Home

Brick, cement, walls and hallways make it tough for your WiFi to travel throughout your home. With Unifi access points, that won't be an issue.

Multi-story Home

Floors and ceilings also pose a problem, obstructing your bandwidth and making connectivity challenging. With Unifi access points, that problem is mute.

L-shaped Home

Unconventional angles or floor plans are no longer a challenge with the superb power of Unifi access points powering your WiFi connection.
